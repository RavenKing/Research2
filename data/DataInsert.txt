3.lec3.jpg
<Capital Market Integration and Wage>
Peter Henry
2011-12-15-13:30
科学会堂
For three years after the typical emerging economy opens its stock market to inflows of foreign capital, the average annual growth rate of the real wage in the manufacturing sector increases by a factor of three. No such increase occurs in a control group of countries that do not liberalize. The temporary increase in wage growth drives up the level of the average worker’s annual compensation by $487 U.S.―an increase equal to nearly one-fifth of their annual pre-liberalization salary. Overall, the results suggest that trade in capital may have a larger impact on wages than trade in goods.

<Reconfigurable Computing C A Mega-Trend in the Future?>
魏少军
2011-12-17-14:00
数学馆201
由于功耗等因素的影响，微处理器架构正面临着重大的变革。“通用为王”是不可逆转的发展大趋势。一方面，CPU和DSP正在向多核（Multi-Cores）和众核（Many-Cores）方向发展，逐渐形成可重构的计算单元阵列，另一方面，现场可编程逻辑阵列（FPGA）不断地集成CPU、DSP和存储器等功能部件，逐渐构筑可重构的逻辑阵列。两者殊途同归，可望在不久的将来逐渐融合，形成统一的动态可重构、可配置、可编程处理器平台。国际上普遍认为，在集成电路工艺进步到16nm后，专用集成电路（ASIC）和SoC将逐渐退出市场，上述动态可重构、可配置、可编程的处理器平台将成为为数不多的主流产品之一，主导电子整机的开发，对计算机架构、软件和最终产品形态以及移动通信终端、便携式电子产品等产生重大和深远的影响。

<On Sinc Discretization and Banded Preconditioning for Linear Third-Order ODEs>
潘建瑜
2011-12-16-15:00
数学馆201
Some draining or coating fluid-flow problems and problems concerning the flow of thin films of viscous fluid with a free surface can be described by third-order ordinary differential equations. In this paper, we solve the boundary value problems of such equations by sinc discretization and prove that the discrete solutions converge to the true solutions of the ordinary differential equations exponentially.
